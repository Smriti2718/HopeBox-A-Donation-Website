Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

It has never been easier to support a charity than in today’s digital age. If you want to make a positive impact, all you need to do is browse fundraisers on hopebox and donate online to an NGO of your choice. From issues such as poverty alleviation to child welfare, you will find thousands of fundraisers that are working in the areas you are passionate about. To donate online, simply search for the NGO or the cause you want to support, make a safe and secure online donation, and receive an official email acknowledgment confirming your donation. After you donate online, you can constantly check an NGO’s page on give.do for updates on the cause you have chosen to support. To donate online, search for causes on hopebox and start your journey to make a positive impact!

When you choose to donate to an NGO on hopebox, every donation counts. Each contribution, small or large, allows NGOs to reach suffering people in need and make a positive impact in their lives. To cover operational costs, there may be a minimum donation amount on some fundraisers, but this amount is small - yet effective. Every donation makes a difference, so whether you are donating to help build a school or purchase medical equipment for a rural hospital, your donation goes far in making a meaningful impact in the lives of so many people in desperate need.

It is safe to make online donations on our website. A secure platform for online transactions has been provided to ensure data security and privacy.

Hopebox was founded with the vision of making philanthropy more accessible to everyone. We saw a gap between people who wanted to help and the organizations that needed funding to create real change. By leveraging technology, we aimed to bridge this gap and provide a seamless way for people to contribute to causes they care about, ensuring that their donations reach the right hands in the most effective way.

Seeing the real-world impact of donations fuels our passion to continue. Every story of a child receiving an education, a family gaining access to healthcare, or a community getting clean water reaffirms our mission. The generosity of donors and the commitment of NGOs drive us forward, inspiring us to keep improving our platform and expanding our reach to support even more causes.

We started Hopebox to democratize charitable giving and make it easy for people from all walks of life to contribute to meaningful causes. We wanted to remove barriers to giving and ensure transparency, so donors know exactly how their contributions are making an impact. By building this platform, we hope to create a community of change-makers who believe in the power of generosity to transform lives.
